YMR 2022 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is YMR?

+ -

The Young Ministers Retreat (YMR) is an annual gathering of young people with a mandate to raise BURNING GIANTS. It is a convocation of young men and women seeking the face of the Lord via the word and in prayers and fasting for 72 hours.

This year, the retreat with the theme "WITNESSES" is projected to host over 200,000 youths and young adults from diverse cities, countries, tribes and tongues, denominations, for one purpose - Enduement with Fire for kingdom dominance.

How accessible is the hostel to the auditorium?

+ -

The hostels are within walking distance. However, there are tricycles and camp shuttle buses if you require one.
